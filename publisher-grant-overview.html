Publisher Grant Overview with Tom Spencer

The Australian Communications and Media Authority (ACMA) recently announced The "Regional Grant Opportunity" which uses uses left-over funding (up to $12.4 million) from round one of the Small and Regional Publishers Innovation Fund.

The fund was established to help regional publishers adapt to the changing media landscape and applications are now open.

Last year, Cognitives and AAP partnered with a number of country newspapers to secure government grants for their digital projects.

We're helping these publishers to implement holistic visions for their digital operations, with website upgrades, paywall integration, print system integration, and most importantly, ongoing strategic consultation.

In this presentation Tom Spencer (Head of Services at Cognitives) provides an overview of the Regional Publisher Grant and our offer.

In the overview Tom:

  • Provides information on eligibility, criteria and general information on the grant
  • Discusses how our suite of digital services can help you meet the fund's requirements
  • Gives you an understanding of how we are currently working with Regional and Small Publishers from the Round 1 of the fund

Our partners had $1.3M worth of grants approved, ranging from $80K-$300K.

It is expected that many of the regional applicants that missed out in Round 1 have a strong chance of succeeding in "Round 1B" with the application process and eligibility criteria being clarified.

Applications for the fund will close on 14 June 2019. Get in touch today.
